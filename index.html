The Jai Shree Group Story

Founded in 1980 on the principles of integrity and quality, Jai Shree Group embarked on a mission to redefine the real estate landscape of Rajasthan. From pioneering residential plotting to developing landmark commercial hubs, we have grown into a diversified powerhouse synonymous with trust and architectural excellence.

Our expansion into turnkey interiors was a natural evolution, driven by our commitment to provide seamless, end-to-end solutions for our discerning clientele. Today, our success is a testament to the enduring relationships we've built with our clients, partners, and the community we proudly serve.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To engineer, build, and design spaces of exceptional quality and enduring value that exceed client expectations, while upholding the highest standards of ethics and sustainability.

Our Vision: To be India’s most trusted and innovative real estate and interiors group, recognized for creating iconic landmarks and enriching communities for generations to come.

1980 - The Beginning

Jai Shree Group is founded, starting with residential plotting projects.

1995 - Expansion

Expanded into constructing luxury flats and commercial properties.

2005 - Interiors Division

Launched a full-service interior design wing to offer turnkey solutions.

2024 - A Trusted Name

Recognized as a leading name with over 1155 successful projects.

Our Spectrum of Services

Delivering end-to-end excellence in real estate and interior design, meticulously tailored to your vision.

Real Estate Development

We manage the entire lifecycle of property development with precision and expertise, navigating regulatory landscapes to deliver projects on time and within budget, ensuring maximum ROI for our stakeholders.

  • Residential & Commercial Construction: Building modern homes and state-of-the-art commercial spaces.
  • Colony & Township Development: Creating vibrant, well-planned communities from the ground up.
  • Infrastructure Projects: Expertise in road construction and other large-scale civil engineering works.

Turnkey Interior Solutions

Our interior design division offers a seamless, hassle-free experience, transforming your vision into reality. We manage every detail from concept to completion, delivering bespoke spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

  • Residential Interiors: Crafting beautiful and functional interiors for flats, houses, and villas.
  • Commercial & Institutional Interiors: Designing innovative spaces for offices, hospitals, universities, and retail.
  • Complete Turnkey Projects: From concept to completion, we manage every aspect of your interior project.

Our Esteemed Clientele

We are proud to have forged lasting partnerships with industry leaders and prestigious organizations.

Building a Better Tomorrow

A Social Commitment by the Mangilal Suthar Charitable Trust

We believe that true success is measured by the positive impact we create. Our commitment extends beyond construction to building stronger communities, fostering sustainable environments, and empowering future generations through focused initiatives.

Education & Empowerment

We operate dedicated hostels for underprivileged children, providing not just safe housing but also comprehensive educational support to empower them for a brighter future.

Gausala (Cow Shelter)

Honoring cultural values by caring for cows with shelter, food, and medical attention, promoting ethical animal welfare.

Educational Scholarships

Empowering the next generation of leaders by offering scholarships to bright, deserving children from low-income families.

Green Initiatives

Our ambitious environmental goal is to plant over 10,000 trees across Rajasthan. This initiative aims to restore ecological balance, improve air quality, and create a sustainable green legacy.

Commitment to Sustainability

Beyond philanthropy, we integrate sustainable practices into our core operations. We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint by implementing efficient waste management systems, prioritizing water conservation, and sourcing eco-friendly materials wherever possible. We believe that responsible building is the only way to build for the future.
